Description:

The representation illlustrates the total sales in different upazilas for the years 2020 and 2022. Basically, Upazila's and their corresponding sales in the year 2022 and 2020. This is a pre and post covid analysis. Each row corresponds to a specific upazila, the respective year, and the total sales in that combination. The upazilas include places like Uttara, Gournadi, Mirpur-1, Mohammadpur, Megna, Agargaon, Mogbazar, Bashundhara R/A, Shyamoli, Farmgate, Keranigong, Kharpara, Savar, Dhanmondi, and Bishwanath. The sales data varies significantly across different upazilas and years, providing a comprehensive overview of the distribution.

A detailed analysis reveals interesting patterns in total sales across upazilas and years. For instance, Mirpur-1 experienced a substantial increase in sales from 2020 to 2022, reaching 200,939.00. Similarly, Savar saw a notable surge in sales, reaching 224,260.00 in 2022. On the other hand, some upazilas, like Shyamoli and Farmgate, experienced a decrease in sales between the two years. This allows for a comprehensive examination of sales trends in specific regions, aiding in identifying growth areas and potential areas for improvement.

The visualization such as graphs depicting upazila vs. total sales with legends for the years 2020 and 2022 provides a visual representation of the sales distribution. This visualization can offer valuable insights into regional sales performance over time, facilitating strategic decision-making for businesses. Identifying upazilas with significant growth or decline in sales allows businesses to tailor marketing strategies or focus more on those zones and target specific areas for expansion or improvement.
